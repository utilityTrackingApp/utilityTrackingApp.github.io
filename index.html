Paying your bills on time is an important aspect of taking control of your financial life. Knowing when your bills are due and making a habit of paying them by the deadline can reduce your stress, save you money, boost your credit score, and enable you to get lower-interest credit in the future. This can be done and managed by the use of utility tracking app where all your bills are tracked before the deadline.

OBJECTIVES

-The purpose of this project is to develop a tracking and storage system for all utilities that a household subscribes to. -It should be able to save the account numbers and passwords if any for each utility one uses. -The user should receive notifications when bills are almost due so that they can plan accordingly. -The system should store and show past expenditure on the utilities and the perpetual changes overtime. -This helps people understand if their usage is going up, down or is constant so that they can act accordingly. -The system could display the projected expenditure for upcoming months to help people budget.
